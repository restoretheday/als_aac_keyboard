## The One Big Thing Left: Install Xcode

**Xcode is like a toolbox for making iPhone and iPad apps.** You need it to test your app on a phone simulator.

### How to Install Xcode:

1. **Open the App Store** on your Mac (the blue icon with an "A")

2. **Search for "Xcode"** in the search bar

3. **Click the "Get" button** (or "Install" if you see that)

4. **Wait patiently** â°
   - This is a BIG download (over 5 GB)
   - It might take 30-60 minutes depending on your internet
   - You can keep using your computer while it downloads

5. **After it finishes downloading:**
   - Open Xcode from your Applications folder
   - It will ask to install extra tools - click "Install"
   - Wait for that to finish
